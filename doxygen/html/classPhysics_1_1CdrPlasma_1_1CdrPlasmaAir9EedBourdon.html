Detailed Description

9-species plasma model for air (79/21 N2/O2 ratio) at 300K

This class uses functions from BOLSIG+ to compute ionization and transport coefficients for air at room temperature. BOLSIG+ has been pre-run for an Electron energy range of \([10^{-3}\textrm{eV}, 20\textrm{eV}]\) which corresponds to Electron temperatures in the range of 1K to 170000K. If you need transport data for other mixing ratios and gas temperatures, you should really, really re-run BOLSIG before running the kinetics in this program.

Routine intended for advancing a reaction network over a time a_dt.

This routine assumes that the subsequent advance is in the form phi^(k+1) = phi^k + S*a_dt. Thus, this routine exists such that users can EITHER fill a_cdrSources and a_rteSources directly with an explicit rule, OR they can perform a fully implicit advance within this routine and set S from that.

[in]a_rteDensitiesGrid-based densities for photons.
