Detailed Description

template<typename P = TracerParticleSolver<TracerParticle<2, 0>>, typename F = FieldSolverMultigrid>
class Physics::StreamerInception::StreamerInceptionStepper< P, F >

Class for volume evaluation of the streamer inception integral.

P is the tracer particle solver and F is the field solver type.
