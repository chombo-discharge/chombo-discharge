Supported propagators for hybrid tau leaping.

These leap propagators are as follows: TauPlain = Regular tau leaping. TauMidpoint = Gillespie's midpoint method TauPRC = Hu and Li's Poisson random correction leap method TauGRC1 = Hu and Li's Gaussian random correction leap method
